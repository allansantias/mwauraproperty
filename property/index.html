Frequently Asked Questions

What documentation will I receive after purchase?

Upon completion of payment, you will receive a fully processed title deed in your name. We handle all the legal transfer processes to ensure a smooth transition of ownership.

Are there any hidden costs?

No. The price we quote includes the cost of the land and all legal transfer fees. You may incur additional costs for land survey if you choose to subdivide the property later.

How do I verify the authenticity of the title deed?

We encourage all our clients to conduct due diligence. You can verify the authenticity of the title deed at the Ministry of Lands offices or through your lawyer. We also provide all necessary documentation for verification.

Do you offer site visits?

Yes, we organize site visits every weekend. You can also schedule a private site visit on any day of the week with our sales team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, M-Pesa, and banker's checks. We do not accept cash payments for security reasons.
